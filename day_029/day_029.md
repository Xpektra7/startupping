## 4. Circuit scenario + SPICE
Scenario:
A differential amplifier for high-side current sensing.

Post idea:
High-side current sensing places the shunt resistor between the battery positive terminal and the load, which requires the amplifier to handle high common-mode voltages.

A practical circuit uses a differential amplifier or dedicated current-sense amplifier with tight resistor matching. SPICE can be used to study common-mode rejection, gain accuracy, offset voltage effects, and output linearity as the common-mode voltage varies with the battery pack voltage.

## 6. Analog IC datasheet analysis
Suggested IC: `INA826`

Technical idea:
The `INA826` is a precision instrumentation amplifier suitable for high-side current sensing and differential voltage measurement in EV BMS applications.

Key datasheet parameters to analyze:
- Common-mode rejection ratio vs. frequency
- Input offset voltage and drift
- Gain accuracy and gain drift
- Bandwidth vs. gain setting
- Input bias current and input impedance
- Supply range and quiescent current

In high-side sensing, the INA826 must reject large common-mode voltage variations while preserving the small differential shunt voltage with high precision.
