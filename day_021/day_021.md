## 3. Market the hardware product
Product: `EV Battery Management System (EV BMS)`

Post idea:
An EV BMS is the safety layer between the battery cells and the rest of the vehicle.

It continuously measures key parameters and decides when to balance, protect, or alert the system.

Without a high-quality BMS, even the best cells cannot perform reliably over time.

## 4. Circuit scenario + SPICE
Scenario:
A galvanic isolation barrier for high-voltage battery sensing.

Post idea:
In an EV BMS, the monitoring circuitry must be isolated from the high-voltage domain for safety and noise immunity.

A practical circuit uses an isolation amplifier or a digital isolator to transfer the analog measurement across the isolation barrier. SPICE can be used to model the isolation transfer function, common-mode transient immunity (CMTI), and signal integrity across the barrier.

This is critical because ground loops and high-voltage transients can destroy sensitive monitoring electronics if isolation is not properly designed.

## 6. Analog IC datasheet analysis
Suggested IC: `ISO124`

Technical idea:
The `ISO124` is a precision isolation amplifier that can be evaluated for galvanically isolating analog measurement paths in an EV BMS.

Key datasheet parameters to analyze:
- Isolation voltage rating and leakage current
- Nonlinearity and gain error across the barrier
- Input and output offset voltage drift
- Bandwidth and slew rate
- Common-mode rejection at high frequency
- Power supply rejection across isolation

In an EV BMS, these parameters determine measurement integrity during fast switching events and under sustained high-voltage stress.
