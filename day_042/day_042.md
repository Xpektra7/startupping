## 3. Market the hardware product
Product: `EV Battery Management System (EV BMS)`

Post idea:
In multi-cell battery packs, the BMS must measure each cell individually while handling the high common-mode voltage of the series stack.

This requires analog multiplexing or dedicated cell monitoring ICs.

Proper sequencing and settling time management ensure accurate readings across all cells.

## 4. Circuit scenario + SPICE
Scenario:
A high-voltage analog multiplexer for scanning series battery cells.

Post idea:
Measuring each cell in a series stack requires an analog multiplexer that can handle the full pack voltage while switching between cell tap points.

A practical circuit uses a high-voltage multiplexer IC followed by a differential amplifier. SPICE can be used to study the switching transients, settling time, on-resistance effects, and charge injection when switching between adjacent cell voltages.

The multiplexer topology trades off component count against measurement rate and accuracy.

## 6. Analog IC datasheet analysis
Suggested IC: `ADG5408`

Technical idea:
The `ADG5408` is a high-voltage 8-channel analog multiplexer that can be evaluated for cell voltage scanning in EV BMS designs.

Key datasheet parameters to analyze:
- Supply voltage range and signal voltage range
- On-resistance and on-resistance flatness
- Switching time and transition glitch
- Charge injection and settling time
- Leakage current over temperature
- Break-before-make switching behavior

In a cell scanning application, the multiplexer must switch cleanly between high-impedance cell taps without introducing measurement errors from charge injection or crosstalk.
