# Day 51 of 100 — Customer Personae (4 Behavioral Segments)

## 1. Four Customer Personae

### Persona 1: "The Burned Rancher" (Primary)
*Behavioral segment: Reactive, cost-driven, peer-validated*

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Tom |
| **Age** | 58 |
| **Location** | Cochise County, AZ |
| **Land** | 3,200 acres, cattle |
| **Pain trigger** | Just repaired a cut fence for $1,200 and lost a $800 calf |
| **Digital profile** | Android user, SMS-only, checks phone 4x/day |
| **Buying motivation** | "I need this to stop costing me money." |
| **Decision process** | Must see it work at a neighbor's ranch first. Will not buy on promise. |
| **Price ceiling** | $50/mo after proof |
| **Objection** | "How do I know it's not just another gadget that dies in a week?" |
| **Best pitch** | "Tom, your fence was cut 4 times last year. Total cost: $4,700. My sensor costs $29/mo and texts you the moment someone crosses. Test it free for 30 days. If it doesn't catch a real event, send it back." |

## 2. Segmentation Summary

| Persona | Urgency | Budget | Tech comfort | Sales cycle |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Burned Rancher | Immediate | $30–50/mo | Low | Short (after proof) |
| The Sheriff | High (mission) | $499/mo agency | Low-Medium | Medium (pilot → contract) |
| The Conservationist | Moderate | $10–15/mo | High | Long (grant cycle) |
| Security Land Manager | Low (preventive) | $30–50/mo | High | Short (direct buy) |

**Go-to-market priority:** Burned Rancher (fastest sale, highest pain, shortest cycle). Sheriff for revenue scale. Conservationist for mission alignment and grant funding.

## 3. Prospecting messages
For Mark (Persona 4 — early adopter):
`"You already secure your house. Your land is 1,800 acres of blind spot. Our sensor texts you when someone crosses — no false alarms, no batteries to change, $29/mo. Want a demo unit?"`
